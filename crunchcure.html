We are a collaborative platform connecting people who optimize their lifestyle through data collection to people who code to extract insights from data.

Using CrunchCure you can combine health data from all your favorite health apps and wearables to start making the most out of your lifestyle!

Tailored insights through
customizable dashboards.

Each individual is unique and therefore requires a particular approach to lifestyle optimization. Does temperature affect your sleep cycles? Does daily meditation or mindfulness reduce your daily stress levels? Do your daily steps influence your mood and general well-being?

The greater the variety of data collected, the greater the variety of investigations you can commence. Tailor the dashboard to your preferences and generate insights that you deem valuable and desired.

Inclusivity

Allows for the integration of a wide array of smart devices.

Connect your tracking devices to CrunchCure to increase the number of derivable insights. Think about smartwatches, smartphones and other novel hardware with data collection features. Fully scalable as new hardware enters the market.

Flexibility

Create your own visualizations.

Tech-savvy individuals are able to create their own data visualizations in Python (using Google Colab) and share this with the community. In the future, we will focus on reducing the required programming knowledge to create your own visualizations.

Data Security

You are in control of your data.

CrunchCure stores all your data in your secure Google drive account so you are always 100% in control of your data. Want to add or remove data? No problem! Want to limit accessibility? Sure thing! No more sleepless nights due to fear for data breaches - we've got it covered!
