Who we are

Cape AI was founded at the beginning of 2019 by enthusiastic data-driven entrepreneur Pieter Boon and Artificial Intelligence expert Koen Bonenkamp. At Cape AI we believe that skilled AI developers can contribute to a better planet. Together with our clients we shape their future and help improve their triple-bottom-line: People, Planet, Profit. What sets us apart from other consulting firms is that all team members can spend 20% of their time on personal development and prototyping disruptive AI-based business models.

What we’re looking for

Problem solver with strong soft skills. Lots of hands-on experience with scalabele data environments, spark, handling SQL databases, docker, cloud, continuous integration, testing and production analytics environments. Very proficient in at least one programming language (preferably Python). Devops skills such as working with git / virtual machines in the cloud. Being able to understand and explain scalability concepts such as parallel computing / indexing etc.

Work through complex business problems and effectively communicate recommendations

Experience with machine learning models in production environments

Solid understanding of cloud environments, databases and platforms such as Spark and Hadoop

Programming skills


Applicant requirements

Finalized university degree in Computer science or similar

Work visa for the location you are applying for (to become eligible for an inter-company transfer, you need to first work 6 months at your local office)

Full-time available and willing to travel to the client


Compensation

Depending on work experience:
South Africa: R 20.000 / R 100.000 month
The Netherlands: EUR 2.500 / EUR 8.000 month
