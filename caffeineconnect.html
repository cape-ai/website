CaffeineConnect analyses digital conversations.

The artificial intelligence engine identifies topics that are discussed in your emails, chats & social media posts. It then predicts which colleagues should be connected over a cup of coffee to prevent reinventing the wheel or to inspire colleagues to organize a team building & knowledge sharing session.

Privacy first

Employees decide which conversations they want to be analyzed.

Peace of mind

The software is ISO certified and runs in isolation within your own cloud / IT infrastructure.

Connection rules

Both colleagues first approve the connection before their contact details are revealed.
