What we look for

We are continuously hiring the
most ambitious people in AI.

We are looking for people who can bring new perspectives and life experiences to our teams. We value curiosity, passion and a desire to learn and love big thinkers that are doers. We are committed to equal employment opportunity regardless of race, religion, sex, sexual orientation, age, citizenship, disability or gender identity.

Personal growth

We spend at least 20% of our time on development.

All employees spend fridays on launching exciting AI-driven prototypes or studying to improve soft and programming skills.

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world

Exchange program

Work at both our Dutch and South African offices

Exciting technology

Launch

Application process (technical positions)

1

Apply.

We'll review your CV and motivation and invite you to fill out a questionnaire.

2

Assessment.

You take a 5-hour assessment which you can do from home. It will test your data science & programming skills.

3

Technical interview.

The last step of the process is a technical interview to assess your understanding of machine learning techniques.

Our core values

Trust

We value open communication, honesty and trust. We are a flat organization - everyone's opinion always matters.

Excellence

Your continuous personal growth is essential to provide our clients with top expertise. We give you all the tools and mentorship to develop yourself as a tech expert or leader in the field.

Entrepreneurship

Our people take initiative. We love bold ideas, new adventures and a laser-sharp focus on getting things done.

Fun

We often hike, braai, have drinks, play ping-pong, make music and organize datathons.

Company culture is our number one priority.

We strive for steep learning curves, fun and impacts for good
