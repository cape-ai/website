The process

We always deliver a tailored solution. To us, a data solution is only ready when it's deployed to production and creates business value.
1

Introduction

We would love to hear more about your challenges and ambitions. During this conversation we'll explain our way of working.

2

Exploration study (optional)

For a fixed price (for 1-4 weeks), we can help you better define your problem by interrogating the data and interviewing stakeholders.

3

Delivery

We can often start within a month and are very flexible with hiring terms. We guarantee the best possible quality.

Working remotely -effective and secure.

We can work with you on premises in The Netherlands, Cape Town, Stellenbosch or Johannesburg, but many of our projects are (partially) remote. We have extensive experience managing remote projects effectively and securely, in your cloud and according to your IT security protocols. Our team members can work with your team in VR (requires a VR device).
