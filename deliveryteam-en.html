The process

We always deliver a tailored solution, to us a data solution is only ready when it's deployed to production and creates business value.
1

Introduction

We love to hear more about your challenges and ambitions. During this conversation we'll explain about our way of working.

2

Exploration study (optional)

For a fixed price (1-4 weeks) we help you define your problem better by looking at the data and interviewing stakeholders.

3

Delivery

We can often start within a month and are very flexible with hiring terms. We guarantee the best quality you can get.

Working remotely, effective and secure.

We work with you on premise in The Netherlands, Cape Town, Stellenbosch and Johannesburg. Though many of our projects are (partially) remote. We have a lot of experience managing remote projects effectively and securely, in your cloud and according to your IT security protocols.
Our team members can work with your team in VR (requires a VR device).
